Solving the challenges in drone-to-edge communications

How do we achieve the two properties, low-latency and high-quality, in drone-to-edge communications for a better applications of drones in mission-criticals tasks?

In recent years, the emerging need for drones in mission-critical tasks, such as search and rescue, brings challenging requirements for video streaming between drones and the edge. The two major requirements for such streaming are high quality and low latency. In this project, we will build a 5G-based drone video analytics system in the scenario of object detection tasks, emphasizing these two properties, as well as achieving a high object detection accuracy.
